Frequently Asked Questions

What is TSA?

TSA in full stands for Technology Students Association. It is an Association of Moi University school of Engineering.

Who does TSA represent?

TSA represents the interest of the entire school of engineering student who are constitutionally members by defaut, and any other student members as constitutionally rightful.

What are the roles of TSA in serving students interest?

1. Protect the rights of the members.
2. Honour members making outstanding contribution.
3. Broaden networks through partnerships.
4. Improve student lectures relationship
5. Ensure equitable gender participation in students affairs.
6. Encourage students to participate in creative and innovative research, symposiums, seminars and conferences locally and internationally.
